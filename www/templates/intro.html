Let me show you how inTandem works...
Mario is an Italian student who wants to learn Japanese...
He loves to meet new people so he decides to look for a linguistic tandem.
He is studying at the university and can go out and meet people only in the evening.
He loves to talk about cooking...
...and always posts photos of his recipes on his Facebook and Instagram page
inTandem is able to look at Mario's profiles on Social Networks and retrieve all information...
...and listen to other users' profiles to find interesting activities and events they are talking about in his area!
With inTandem Mario can find the best linguistic tandem matching his skills, interests and calendar...
without spending a minute writing boring descriptions, searching people with the same interests or arranging meetings!
